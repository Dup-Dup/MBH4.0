Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for both future members and future investors.

Is this a dating app or a marriage app?

MuslimBetterHalf is built for Nikah, not casual dating. Every product decision is aligned with long-term, intentional marriage — not quick swipes or endless chatting.

How do you protect women on the platform?

Women can control who sees their photos, use blur and hidden modes, and enable guardian involvement. Combined with human verification, this creates a safer, higher-trust environment.

What makes your verification different from other apps?

We combine ID upload with human call-center verification — not just automated checks. This reduces fake profiles and builds a community where people can genuinely trust who they are talking to.

When will the app be fully launched?

We are currently in the early-access waitlist stage. Our first public release will prioritize verified, high-intent users from the waitlist, followed by regional rollouts.

Why is this an attractive opportunity for investors?

MuslimBetterHalf sits at the intersection of a large, underserved market and a trust-critical category. Our moat is built on human verification, women-first safety, and deep alignment with Islamic values.

How will you scale verification globally?

Our roadmap includes regional verification hubs, training playbooks, and a mix of internal teams and vetted partners to maintain quality, compliance, and scalability.
